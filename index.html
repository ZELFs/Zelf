Distributed Webs Development

Scuttlebutt (SSB) is a decentralised offline-first communication protocol, known as the gossip protocol. SSB has offline-first qualities and is found on Internet layers 4-7. I have been a face outward for the movement of around 20,000 people globally for four years.

As part of the Handshake Council I was as lead designer of the community budgeting process, the Handshake Councils sole task. The budget was of 100,000€ in which the entire community had an opportunity to participate through a physically and digitally blended process.

As the project manager for the NGI Pointer project of €200,000 I supported with frames for developing the back-end code base of Scuttlebutt together with a core team of 6 others. The project also involved a research team of 6 externally who supported in the development of the protocol design. Read more about NGI and the project here.
